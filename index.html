About

Hi, I’m Pranjal Jain, a photographer based in Indore, India, with a deep passion for visual storytelling. My journey with photography began in childhood—capturing moments on school trips, drawn to nature, people, and places. Over time, what started as a fascination turned into a profession that allows me to blend creativity with meaningful narratives. ​ With a background in Interior Design and a master’s degree in Public Space Design, I bring a unique perspective to photography, particularly in architecture, street photography, and candid moments. During my master’s studies, I refined my eye for detail, composition, and emotion—elements that now define my work. ​ Since 2020, I have worked with businesses, brands, and individuals, helping them express their identity through compelling visuals. My expertise extends beyond photography—I also create engaging digital content for Instagram, blending art and strategy to enhance online presence. For me, photography is more than just an image—it’s a story waiting to be told. Whether it’s capturing a fleeting expression, the rhythm of the streets, or the essence of a brand, I believe in creating visuals that speak louder than words.

Capturing Stories, Emotions & Spaces.

At Chitrapeti, we bring a documentary photographer’s eye to every moment — preserving genuine emotions, raw expressions, and the character of each space we frame. Our work spans a wide range of genres including candid wedding and pre-wedding shoots, architectural and interior photography, immersive travel imagery, and dynamic street scenes. We’re also deeply committed to documenting the intimate journey from maternity to baby, celebrating life’s most tender phases.
